Hello | 您好 | こんにちは | 안녕하세요

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Networks at Kyushu Institute of Technology (Kyutech), where I lead the Intelligent Networked Systems Laboratory (INSysLab) focusing on cutting-edge research in intelligent and networked systems. Before joining Kyutech, I worked as a JSPS International Research Fellow at Kyushu University, collaborating closely with Prof. Osamu Muta. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Korea University, under the supervision of Prof. Wonjun Lee.

I am broadly interested in diverse networked systems, from the basic architecture of the physical layer to high-level applications. I am also a workout enthusiast. Running, tennis, and badminton are some of my favorite categories.
